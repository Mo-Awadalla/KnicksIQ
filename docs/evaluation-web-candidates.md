These are question and fact candidates found during harness construction. They
are not gold labels until a reviewer confirms the same facts in a pinned
KnicksIQ release and maps its evidence IDs.

- **December 2, 2025 at Boston:** Boston won 123-117. Jaylen Brown scored 42,
  and Boston stopped a New York rally that cut a 15-point deficit to 102-99.
  Candidate questions: “How did Boston stop the Knicks' comeback?” and “What
  happened after New York cut the lead to three?”
  Source: https://www.espn.com/nba/recap/_/gameId/401810175
- **February 8, 2026 at Boston:** New York won 111-89. Jalen Brunson scored 31,
  Josh Hart scored 19, and the Knicks did not trail over the final 42 minutes.
  Candidate questions: “When did the Knicks take control against Boston?” and
  “Compare Brunson and Hart in the February Boston win.”
  Source: https://www.espn.com/nba/recap/_/gameId/401810612
- **April 9, 2026 vs. Boston:** New York won 112-106. Josh Hart scored 15 of his
  26 points in the fourth quarter; Jalen Brunson had 25 points and 10 assists.
  Candidate questions: “Who drove the fourth-quarter win over Boston?” and
  “How did Hart's fourth quarter compare with the rest of his game?”
  Source: https://www.espn.com/nba/recap?gameId=401811023
