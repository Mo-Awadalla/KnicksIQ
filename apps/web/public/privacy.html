Privacy

KnicksIQ has no accounts and sets no cookies. Analyst conversations remain in browser memory and disappear on reload. The service records only anonymous aggregate events: feature or page, intent category, retrieval route, outcome, latency bucket, and citation click. It does not retain prompts, IP addresses, headers, tokens, evidence bodies, or provider payloads.

Feedback is handled by Formspree. If you submit feedback, Formspree receives the fields you choose to send and may retain free-tier submissions for 30 days. Reply email is optional and requires consent.
