Sources and limitations

The archive is built offline from NBA game finder, play-by-play, traditional box-score, and game-summary data. Each public release is checksummed and activated only after scores, dates, opponents, period totals, team totals, player totals, play-by-play, and reviewed reports reconcile.

Citations identify the game and available source metadata. This archive does not provide live scores, injuries, trades, standings, future games, or claims that cannot be supported by its factual data.

NBA and team names and marks belong to their respective owners. Legal review of upstream and provider terms is required before launch.
